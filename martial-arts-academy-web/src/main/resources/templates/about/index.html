Martial arts academy is a martial arts dojo founded in 1994 by three of our top trainers: Misha Nurmagomedov, Bruno Gracie and Idris Van Damme. Their goal was establishing a place in which young genereations can develop themselves into professional fighters , but most importantly into good people. Their doctrine wasn't about violence as many would instantly think upon mentioning martial arts but respect, discipline and helping those in need. Their valuable martial arts knowledge is passed on younger generations building a community of strong , helpful and disciplined people. Wheter you have professional or amateur ambitions you can join our team and explore the faboulous world of martial arts.
